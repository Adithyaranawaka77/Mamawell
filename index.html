Project ID: R25-017 • AI for Maternal Wellness

MamaWell – AI-Driven Maternal Wellness System

An integrated research platform that analyzes medical reports, forecasts risks, and provides trimester-aware guidance for healthier pregnancies.

Explore Modules Request a Demo

Project Overview

Goal

Deliver personalized, technology-driven support for pregnant mothers by combining data-driven risk insights, nutrition, exercise, and a supportive assistant.

Why Now

Risk detection is often delayed and guidance is generic. MamaWell unifies clinical indicators with tailored recommendations to support timely decisions.

Scope

Focus on trimester-wise report analysis, risk forecasting (e.g., GDM, anemia), meal planning, exercise recommendations, and an AI assistant.

Problem & Scope

Challenges

Delayed detection of risks; fragmented information across records; limited access to personalized guidance—especially beyond urban centers.

Gap

Most apps offer general tips, not data-backed, trimester-specific insights tied to clinical measures like BMI, BP, Hb, or glucose trends.

Approach

MamaWell analyzes uploaded reports, forecasts risk levels, and offers nutrition & exercise plans with a 24/7 support assistant.

Objectives

Main Objective

Provide a comprehensive, personalized maternal-wellness experience through AI-assisted analysis and recommendations.

O1 – Report Analysis

Automate trimester-wise extraction and interpretation of key biomarkers from medical reports.

O2 – Risk Forecasting

Predict conditions such as gestational diabetes and anemia using robust ML ensembles.

O3 – Support Assist

Offer an always-available assistant that answers common questions and supports record-keeping.

O4 – Lifestyle Guidance

Generate personalized meal plans and safe, trimester-aware exercise recommendations.

Methodology

Data Pipeline

  • Collect → Clean → Split (trimester-wise records and supportive datasets)
  • Feature engineering (BMI bands, risk indicators) & normalization
  • Ethics: consent, anonymization, and secure handling

Modeling & Evaluation

  • Baseline models → boosted ensembles (e.g., gradient boosting)
  • Cross-validation; metrics such as F1 and ROC-AUC
  • Error analysis and bias checks; iterative refinement

System Modules

Pregnancy Risk Forecasting

Integrates patient records (BMI, BP, Hb, glucose trends) to flag elevated risk levels early.

Pregnancy Support Assist

AI assistant (text/voice ready) for FAQs, education, and guided self-tracking.

Personalized Meal Planning

Trimester-specific plans aligned to risk category, preferences, and nutritional goals.

Risk-Aware Exercise

Safe, trimester-aware exercise suggestions tailored to health indicators.

Dashboard & Alerts

Longitudinal visualization, progress insights, and proactive notifications.

Integration

Modular architecture for mobile/clinic interfaces and future clinical validation.
