Problem & Scope

Context

Early identification of risks (e.g., GDM, anemia) is critical, yet mothers often lack proactive tools.

Gap

Few integrated, locally validated solutions combine report analysis with personalized insights.

Solution

MamaWell analyzes trimester-wise reports, predicts risk, and offers tailored guidance with an assistant.

Objectives

O1

Automate trimester-wise medical report analysis.

O2

Predict gestational diabetes & anemia with ML ensembles.

O3

Deploy a 24/7 pregnancy support assistant.

O4

Provide a dashboard for longitudinal risk tracking.
